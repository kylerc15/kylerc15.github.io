Target Audience

The target audience for this page are farmers. Especially farmers who's crops or farm land is affected by the weather and need to plan ahead according to the upcoming forecast.

Personas

Potato farmer Joe Jolley

Joe is a potato farmer in Preston, Idaho. Him and his wife have been farming for years. All of his children are grown up and moved out. He runs the farm with his wife. He likes to look ahead for the upcoming weather so he can know how to treat his farm to get the best potatoes that he can for his buyers. He also needs to know because he is close to retirement. His body is getting worn out from all the years of farming. He needs to prepare himself for bad weather, or know if he just can't go out at all.

Gardner Sally Sherman

Sally gardens different types of fruits and vegetables. She has her own business where she sells her products for extra money. Her husband makes plenty of money so its more for her own pleasure. She has 4 young children, The oldest two are 10 and 12. She wants to teach them her gardening skills so that they can help her and maybe even do it on their own one day. The poeople of her Idaho love her products and she is becoming more and more popular. She needs to know the weather because if it is too cold or too hot she just won't take her kids out or maybe even stay home herself.

Scenarios

  • How cold will the weather be for my plants?
  • Based on the heat levels, how much water do my crops need?
  • What kind of clothing do I need to wear when I go work on my garden?
  • Will there be a frost tonight?
  • What does the weather look like for the upcoming week?
